About

Marco Anthony Archer, also known as Phora is an American rapper from Anaheim California who was born on October 11, 1994. In the year 2017 on February 14th, Phora signed with Warner Bros. Records, but later down the line in July of 2019, he split away from Warner Bros. Records and created his own label by the name, Yours Truly in 2011. Phora writes albums has written songs that which relates his past struggles and depression. These albums include Still a Kid, One Life to Live, Sincerely Yours, Nights Like These, Angels with Broken Wings, With Love, Heartbreak Hotel, and his debut album Yours Truly Forever. Phora has sang along with some top artists in the music career such as Jhene Aiko, Kehlani, and Toosii.

Prior to his music career, Marco worked as a tattoo artist which aided in the funding of his upcoming music career. Marco has survived a stabbing and two shootings; on August 25, 2015, Marco was shot by .45 caliber pistol, a total of 3 bullets were fired into his neck and back, missing his vertebra.
